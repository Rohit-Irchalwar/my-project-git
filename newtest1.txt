Boruto: Naruto Next Generations[d] is a Japanese manga series written by Ukyō Kodachi 
and Masashi Kishimoto, and illustrated by Mikio Ikemoto. It began monthly serialization with Kodachi
 as writer and Kishimoto as editorial supervisor in Shueisha's shōnen manga magazine, Weekly Shōnen Jump, in May 2016 and was 
transferred to Shueisha's monthly magazine, V Jump, in July 2019. In November 2020 Kodachi stepped down, with Kishimoto 
taking over as writer.[2] Boruto is a spin-off and a sequel to Kishimoto's Naruto, 
which follows the exploits of Naruto Uzumaki's son, Boruto Uzumaki, and his ninja team.
